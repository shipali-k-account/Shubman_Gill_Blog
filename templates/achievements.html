🏅 Achievements of Shubman Gill

💥 Double Century in ODIs

Shubman Gill became the youngest Indian cricketer to score a double century in One Day Internationals, displaying his exceptional talent and maturity at a young age. This milestone cemented his place among India’s batting greats.

🏆 Orange Cap – IPL 2023

In IPL 2023, Shubman’s consistent brilliance earned him the coveted Orange Cap as the highest run-scorer with over 890 runs, captivating fans with his elegant stroke play and resilience.

🌍 U-19 World Cup Champion

As vice-captain, Gill played a crucial role in India’s triumphant campaign at the 2018 ICC U-19 World Cup, showcasing leadership qualities and skill that foretold his future success.

🎖️ Player of the Tournament

His outstanding performances earned him the Player of the Tournament title in the 2018 U-19 World Cup, recognized for his steady batting and game-changing innings under pressure.

📈 Fastest Indian to 2000 ODI Runs

Demonstrating remarkable consistency, Shubman became the fastest Indian to reach 2000 runs in ODIs, reinforcing his reputation as a dependable and prolific batsman.

🏆 ICC Champions Trophy 2025 Winner

Shubman Gill played a pivotal role in India's triumphant campaign, contributing significantly to their third ICC Champions Trophy title. His consistent performances were instrumental in leading the team to victory.

🎯 Historic Edgbaston Performance (July 2025)

Gill became the first player in 148 years to score a double-century and a 150+ innings in the same Test, registering scores of 269 & 161, and totaling 430—an Indian Test record.

📈 India’s Highest Test Runs in a Match

Scored 430 runs at Edgbaston, breaking Sunil Gavaskar’s long-standing record of 344 runs in a single Test.

👑 Highest Test Score by an Indian in England

Gill’s 269 at Edgbaston is now the highest-ever Test score by an Indian in England, surpassing Sunil Gavaskar’s 221 in 1979.
